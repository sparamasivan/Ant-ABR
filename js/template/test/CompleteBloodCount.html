White Blood Cells (WBC)

White blood cells protect and defend {{patient.name}}’s body against "invaders" like viruses, bacteria and parasites. There are different kinds of white blood cells that specialize in fighting different kinds of invaders.

Platelets

Platelets are like natural bandages – they help the body heal. If {{patient.name}} is cut, platelets rush to the site and form a scab to stop the bleeding.
