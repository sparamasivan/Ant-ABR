Kidneys

The Master Filter System

{{patient.name}}’s organs and cells produce waste, which is dumped into the blood. The kidneys filter the blood, returning clean blood to the body and flushing the waste into the bladder.

Bladder

The Holding Tank

The bladder is a holding tank where the body stores its waste, which is then expelled as urine.
