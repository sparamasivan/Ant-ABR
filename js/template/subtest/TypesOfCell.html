Immune Defenders

These cells strengthen the immune system by fighting viral infections and remembering them, so if the virus returns, the cells recognize it and make antibodies to destroy it.

Infection Fighters

These cells fight bacterial infection and inflammation at the site – when you see puss form in a cut that is the infection fighters in action!

Allergen Watchmen

These cells keep a lookout for any allergens that might enter {{patient.name}}’s system. An allergen can be anything, from pollen to heartworm, that causes the body to react, becoming irritated or inflamed.
